Case Study: From Low Visibility to Strong Google Maps Presence

Client: Local wellness service business

The Situation

When the client started working with me, their Google Business Profile was incomplete and not strategically optimised. Categories were not aligned with real search behaviour, services were not structured properly, and local search positioning was weak. As a result, the business was barely appearing for relevant “service + city” searches.

What Was Done

  • Full Google Business Profile audit and optimisation
  • Correct primary and secondary categories
  • Structured services aligned with real search terms
  • Improved business description for local relevance
  • Trust signal optimisation (photos & completeness)
  • Conversion-focused improvements (calls & messaging)

The Result

Within days, the business started appearing for relevant local searches including:

  • Service + City searches
  • “Near me” searches
  • Category-based local queries

The client reported improved visibility, increased profile activity, and stronger professional credibility online.

Client Feedback
