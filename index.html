Why your business needs to show up online

In Jersey, people search on their phone before they call or visit. If your business doesn’t appear – or looks confusing – they move on.

Many solid local businesses still live only on word of mouth. That works – until someone new searches online and can’t find you or doesn’t understand what you do.


A simple website and a small chatbot can explain your services, show you are real and give people one tap to contact you on WhatsApp, without endless back-and-forth.

What a clear website should do

  • Explain in seconds what your business actually offers.
  • Show real services, photos and where you are based.
  • Make it easy to contact you via WhatsApp, call or email.
  • Answer the same basic questions so you don’t have to repeat yourself.
  • Look clean and trustworthy on any phone or laptop.
