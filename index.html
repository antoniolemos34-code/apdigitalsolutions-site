FAQ

Quick answers to reduce friction and help customers say “yes”.

How fast can you deliver?

It depends on scope, but most landing pages can be delivered quickly. Full websites and Shopify builds take longer. We confirm a clear timeline before starting.

Do you handle hosting / domain?

Yes — we can help you connect domain + hosting and publish your website. If you prefer GitHub Pages/Netlify, we can work with that too.

Can you integrate WhatsApp without showing my number?

Your site can use click-to-chat links and buttons. Visitors see WhatsApp as the contact method — your number is not displayed as text on the page.

Do you support Shopify improvements?

Yes. We can improve homepage, collections, product layout, trust, and conversion points — plus add WhatsApp contact and automation options.
