Why most commercial cleaning companies struggle on Google Maps

Many commercial cleaning companies rely on referrals, tender platforms or existing contracts. But when potential clients search for “office cleaning company near me” or “commercial cleaning Birmingham”, they often don’t appear.

Common issues include:

  • Incorrect primary category
  • Weak service area configuration
  • No keyword alignment in services
  • Low review volume or no commercial-focused reviews
  • Poor call and enquiry conversion path

What actually improves Google Maps visibility

1. Correct commercial intent positioning

Your Google Business Profile must reflect commercial intent — office contracts, industrial cleaning, facilities support — not just generic “cleaning services”.

2. Category and service precision

Aligning categories and services with real commercial search queries increases relevance.

3. Review strategy aligned to commercial clients

Reviews mentioning offices, warehouses or contract cleaning improve trust signals and influence conversion.

4. Conversion optimisation

Visibility without action is wasted. We optimise the call and enquiry path so profile views convert into enquiries.
