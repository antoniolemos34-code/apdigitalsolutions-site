Why commercial cleaning GBPs fail

Commercial buyers search differently. If your GBP doesn’t signal “commercial intent”, Google won’t rank you where it matters.

  • Wrong primary category (or weak supporting categories)
  • Services not aligned to real commercial searches
  • Service areas configured in a way that reduces relevance
  • Reviews don’t mention offices/warehouses/contracts
  • Weak call/message path (views don’t become enquiries)

What actually improves Google Maps visibility

Focus on controllable signals: relevance, trust and conversion.

Relevance

Category + services precision

Structure your GBP around commercial searches (office, facilities, industrial/warehouse, contract cleaning).

Trust

Commercial proof

Reviews, photos, updates and completeness increase selection rate — especially when they reference commercial work.

Conversion

Make enquiry frictionless

Tighten the call/message path so people move from “view” to “contact” fast.

FAQs

Quick answers commercial cleaning companies ask before investing.

How long does it take to improve Maps visibility?

Often you’ll see early movement within weeks after key fixes. Stronger positioning usually builds over 1–3 months with consistent optimisation and review activity.

Do we need a website?

A website helps conversion and authority, but you can still improve Maps visibility with GBP optimisation alone. Best results come from both working together.

Do we need Google Ads?

Ads can accelerate enquiries, but they are optional. The foundation is a properly structured GBP that matches commercial intent and converts clicks into enquiries.
